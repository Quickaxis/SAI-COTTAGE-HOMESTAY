Our Story

Welcome to Sai Cottage

Nestled in the quiet residential locality of Boiragimoth in Dibrugarh, Sai Cottage Homestay is born out of our passion for sharing genuine, homelike hospitality. Unlike commercial hotels, we welcome you directly into our peaceful atmosphere, offering a quiet escape from the bustle of the city center.

We prioritize absolute cleanliness, fresh linen, and comfortable stays for all travelers. Whether you are in town for a medical visit to Assam Medical College (AMCH), exploring tea plantations, visiting on business, or traveling with family, we offer the comforts of home—including access to a fully functional common kitchen and a safe, peaceful residential neighborhood.

We offer 8 neatly prepared rooms tailored to fit your budget. Experience simple comfort, warm hospitality, and the true colors of Assam.
